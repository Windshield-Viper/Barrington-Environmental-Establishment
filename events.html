Barrington Environmental Establishment

The BEE hosts a variety of local events aiming to support the East Bay’s local environment. These include speaker events, tree plantings, beach cleanups, and more.

Upcoming events:

April 26 - Community service event with the Barrington Land Conservation Trust @ the Barrington High School Library at 3:15pm. No sign up necessary - just show up! Our next event is on a new community service opportunity for Barrington High School students - the Student Stewardship Team, which works to provide pathways for youth involvement in environmental stewardship.

Projects may include trail maintenance, clean ups, removal of invasive species, or special stewardship projects. In addition, the Team is looking for students to take photographs, produce short videos, assist in creating content for social media, write reflections on issues relating to environmental stewardship and justice, or create other forms of artwork connecting to their experience on the Student Stewardship Team. Positions are available for students 14 and over. You can find a flyer about this event here.
