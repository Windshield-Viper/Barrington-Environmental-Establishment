Barrington Environmental

Establishment


A student-led East Bay nonprofit

The Barrington Environment Establishment is a student-led climate action nonprofit aiming to BEE the change in the East Bay and Rhode Island as a whole.

A Local Impact

"Climate change risks Rhode Island’s natural environment, health, welfare, economic well-being, and more." - RI State Government

We’re in the middle of a climate crisis that'll affect the entire world, including Rhode Island. For us to overcome the challenge, people will have to take action in their communities and beyond, creating waves of positive change. The Barrington Environmental Establishment was created to help the East Bay become more environmentally aware through community events like talks and cleanups as well as a biweekly newsletter. We're looking forward to meeting you!
