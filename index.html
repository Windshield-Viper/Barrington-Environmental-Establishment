Barrington Environmental

Establishment



A student-led East Bay nonprofit

The Barrington Environmental Establishment is a climate action organization aiming to BEE the change in the East Bay and Rhode Island as a whole.

A Local Impact

"Climate change has the potential to pose significant risks for Rhode Island’s... health, welfare, and economic well-being." - State of Rhode Island [1]

We’re in the middle of a climate crisis that'll affect the entire world, including Rhode Island. For us to overcome the challenge, people will have to take action in their communities and beyond, creating waves of positive change. The Barrington Environmental Establishment was created to help the East Bay become more environmentally aware through community events like talks and cleanups as well as a biweekly newsletter. If you'd like to help, consider becoming a member, signing up for our newsletter, or attending one of our events. Thank you!
